## CRISIS COMMUNICATION PLAN

### If Negative Comments/Posts:
1. Respond promptly and professionally
2. Take complex issues offline ("Please email aaron@shellfish-society.org")
3. Never delete unless spam/abuse
4. Acknowledge valid concerns
5. Provide factual information
6. Follow up privately

### If Misinformation About Restoration:
1. Correct politely with credible sources
2. Link to peer-reviewed research
3. Offer to connect with experts
4. Don't engage in arguments
5. Focus on facts, not feelings

### If Technical Issues (Conference, Website):
1. Acknowledge immediately
2. Provide timeline for resolution
3. Update frequently
4. Thank people for patience
5. Explain what happened and how you fixed it
