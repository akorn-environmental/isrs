## How to Get Missing IRS Determination Letter

### Option 1: IRS Online Search (Try First)
1. Go to: https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/tax-exempt-organization-search
2. Search for "International Shellfish Restoration Society" or ISRS EIN
3. If found, download the determination letter

### Option 2: Request from IRS (If not found online)
1. Complete IRS Form 4506-A: https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-4506-a
2. Mail to IRS (address on form)
3. Processing time: 60+ days
4. Cost: Free for organization's own records

### Option 3: Check Organization Records
- Original letter sent when 501(c)(3) status was granted
- May be with incorporation documents
- Check with founding board members or attorney who filed

## MARYLAND - Exempt Organization Fund-Raising Notice

### Filing Information
- **Agency:** Maryland Secretary of State
- **Form:** Exempt Organization Fund-Raising Notice
- **Fee:** FREE (under $25,000 in contributions)
- **Portal:** https://sos.maryland.gov/Charity/Pages/Registering-Charity.aspx
- **Phone:** (410) 974-5521
- **Form Download:** https://sos.maryland.gov/Documents/ExemptOrganizationFund-RaisingNotice.pdf

### Required Information (Pre-filled below)
See "Maryland Form Draft" section below.

### Renewal
- Must be renewed annually
- Due within 6 months of fiscal year end (with automatic extension to 11th month)

## WASHINGTON - Optional Registration

### Filing Information
- **Agency:** Washington Secretary of State, Charities Program
- **Form:** Optional Registration for Charitable Organizations
- **Fee:** FREE or minimal
- **Portal:** https://www.sos.wa.gov/corporations-charities/nonprofits-charities/charitable-organizations
- **Forms Page:** https://www.sos.wa.gov/corporations-charities/nonprofits-charities/charities/all-forms
- **Phone:** (360) 725-0377
- **Email:** charities@sos.wa.gov

### Why File Optional Registration?
- Allows WA Secretary of State to confirm legitimacy to donors
- Builds public trust
- Prepares ISRS if contributions grow above $50,000

### Required Information (Pre-filled below)
See "Washington Form Draft" section below.

### Important 2026 Changes
- Online filing is now MANDATORY
- Email address REQUIRED (rejections starting Jan 20, 2026)
