ORGANIZATION OVERVIEW:
The International Shellfish Restoration Society (ISRS) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 39-2829151) established in 2024 to support the global shellfish restoration community. We emerged from the International Conference on Shellfish Restoration (ICSR), which has convened restoration professionals, scientists, and practitioners since 1996. Our network includes 2,600+ researchers, government agencies, and conservation organizations across six continents working to restore vital oyster, mussel, and clam ecosystems.
