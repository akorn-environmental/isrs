## üöÄ How to Use the AI Assistant

### Opening the Assistant

1. **Look for the AI Assistant tab** on the right side of your screen
   - You'll see a vertical tab with ü§ñ **AI Assistant**

2. **Click the tab** to open the assistant panel

3. **The panel will slide out** showing:
   - Example questions you can ask
   - An input field to type your question
   - An "Ask" button to submit

### Asking Questions

**Option 1: Use Example Questions**
- Click any of the pre-written example questions
- The question will auto-fill in the input field
- Click "Ask" to submit

**Option 2: Type Your Own Question**
- Click in the input field
- Type your question in natural language
- Press Enter or click "Ask"

### Understanding Responses

The AI Assistant provides different types of responses:

- **üìä Metrics**: Statistics and numbers (e.g., "How many registrations do we have?")
- **üìù Lists**: Collections of items (e.g., "Show me contacts from universities")
- **‚ÑπÔ∏è Information**: General answers and explanations
- **üí° Insights**: Recommendations and suggestions

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### "Failed to process query"
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you're logged in
- Try rephrasing your question
- Contact support if the issue persists

### Assistant Not Appearing
- Make sure you're on an admin or member portal page
- Try refreshing the page (Cmd/Ctrl + R)
- Clear browser cache if needed

### Slow Responses
- Complex queries may take 3-5 seconds
- Database searches can take longer
- Be patient - the "Analyzing..." message means it's working
