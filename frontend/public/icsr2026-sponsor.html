Why Sponsor ICSR2026?

ICSR2026 attracts 350+ practitioners from 25+ countries, serving as the global nexus for advancing shellfish restoration science, practice, and networks. Since 1996, this conference has been held worldwide—from Canada to Scotland to Australia—fostering lasting partnerships among practitioners on the leading edge of molluscan shellfish restoration.

Joining us as a sponsor aligns you with organizations like NOAA, Sea Grant, and The Nature Conservancy, who have historically supported ICSR. Your organization will receive extensive recognition throughout the conference and in our publications.
